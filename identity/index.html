Explore your transport emissions

The open source Context of Vehicle Emissions (COVE) Tool has been developed through a collaboration between Kent Community Health NHS Foundation Trust and Loughborough University. Find out how the tool can help you with your analysis.

  • Get Started

Track

Quantify your progression towards a no-emission fleet

Measure

Calculate emissions per journey and per vehicle with the help of Department for Transport data

Discover

Using data visualisation and mapping tools to understand impacts and identify potential solutions

Verify

Verify compliance by checking vehicles claiming mileage are taxed and MOT'd.

Can we help?

While the tool has been developed open source, some data wrangling and python implementation expertise may be needed to use the tool. Please get in touch with the Sustainability Team at Kent Community Health NHS Foundation Trust to learn more about how can support you to better understand your owned, leased and greyfleet.
